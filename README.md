# Power-BI-Dashboard
Dataset 6: India National Family Health Survey (NFHS-5)
Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/kmldas/india-national-family-health-survey-nfhs 
The National Family Health Survey (NFHS-5) provides district-level data on key demographic, health, and nutrition indicators across India. It includes metrics on child and maternal health, education, sanitation, and gender-related indicators, offering insights into social and public health outcomes.
Create a map visual showing district-level performance in one key indicator (e.g., literacy rate, infant mortality rate).
Compare male vs. female literacy rates using a bar chart or KPI visuals.
Display the top 10 and bottom 10 districts for one indicator (e.g., child nutrition, institutional births).
Calculate and visualize the average percentage for key indicators at the state level.
Which states or districts perform consistently well across multiple indicators?
Are there regions that lag significantly? What might explain disparities?
How does women’s literacy correlate with child health or nutrition outcomes?
Examine how sanitation access relates to malnutrition or maternal health.
Identify regional clusters with similar demographic or health profiles.
Investigate whether economic or urbanization factors explain outcomes.
Visualize improvements since NFHS-4 using external data.
What should be top policy priorities for improving health and nutrition?
